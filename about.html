Introduction & Brief History

The Sustainable Development Goals Evaluation Conference & Awards (SUDGEC) is a premier international programme that unites policy makers, development professionals, researchers, and institutions to evaluate global progress towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

With over a decade of impact, SUDGEC has been a driving force in promoting and assessing development-based initiatives, particularly in African countries including Nigeria â€” tracing its roots back to the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) era.

Through annual conferences, evaluations, and collaborative projects, SUDGEC has become a hub for learning, sharing, and joint action toward a sustainable future.

Conference Focus & Awards

The 2025 gathering focuses on key developmental themes such as education, infrastructure, youth empowerment, governance, finance, and leadership. SUDGEC collaborates with governments, communities, and organizations to design and implement impactful projects addressing real development needs.

Outstanding individuals and institutions will be honored with International SDG Awards for their remarkable contributions to sustainable development across Africa and beyond.
