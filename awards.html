Honouring excellence and innovation across Africa and beyond.

Award Categories

Recognizing innovation, excellence, and impact across multiple Sustainable Development domains.

1. SDGs Champion Award

Honouring individuals or institutions that have made significant contributions to achieving the SDGs.

2. Green Economy Award

Recognizing innovative approaches to sustainable economic development.

3. Climate Action Award

Honouring efforts to reduce carbon emissions and mitigate climate change.

4. Education for All Award

Recognizing initiatives that promote access to quality education.

5. Healthcare Innovation Award

Celebrating innovation in healthcare delivery and community wellness.

6. Poverty Reduction Award

Recognizing significant efforts to reduce poverty and promote empowerment.

7. Environmental Conservation Award

Honouring impactful initiatives in environmental protection and climate resilience.

8. Good Governance Award

Recognizing transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership in governance.

9. Science and Technology Award

Honouring innovation in science, technology, and digital transformation.
